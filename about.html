Our Story

BERTALS is a Nigerian Company incorporated with CAC in 2017. BERTALS Nigeria Limited provides world class services in civil, mechanical, electrical works and general procurement for companies and organizations.

BERTALS is registered with NOGIC JQS as a service company in the Oil and Gas industry to provide maintenance services in the Nigerian Content initiatives.

  • We also provide world class quality support services to the Oil and Gas industry companies.
  • BERTALS is up to date in her financial obligation as a tax paying company
  • Ensure quality policy is communicated, understood and available to relevant interested parties within and outside the organization.

We are committed to open dialogue on an ongoing basis, with all our stakeholders - both internal and external. As a company, we recognize that our activities and operations are a legitimate subject for public scrutiny, and we are keen to take an active part in any debate they may cause. Second, we will audit our financial, environmental and social performance on a regular basis, report to our stakeholders annually and subject our reporting to verification and evaluation with the objective of continuous improvement.
